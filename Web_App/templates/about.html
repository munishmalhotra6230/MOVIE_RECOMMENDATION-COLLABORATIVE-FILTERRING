About LIBRARY RAT

Library Rat is your personal book recommendation system that helps readers discover the most popular and highly rated books. Our platform uses user preferences and collaborative filtering to suggest books you will love.

Our mission is to promote reading and make it easier for book lovers to find their next favorite read. Whether you are a casual reader or a bibliophile, Library Rat is here to guide you through the vast world of literature.
